How EXPENSIVE is YOUR COUNTRY?

Living in the 21st century is not easy nor it is cheap. We have to pay for our food, rent, bills, groceries, and many other thing in a daily basis,
and how many of us ask the exact same question each time, "WHY is EVERYTHING SO EXPENSIVE?"
Derived from Numbeo's "Cost of Living Index by Country 2020" , this study could show you how expensive your country is, in terms of cost of living, local purchasing power, restaurant price, groceries, and rent with percentage values in comparison to New York city, USA.
